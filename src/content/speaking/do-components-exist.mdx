It seems like an easy question. But once we look past the surface and begin to gauge impact and adoption, the concept of a component turns out to be harder to identify. Is a card with a button a single component, or a composition of several? How do patterns and styles affect the definition? In this conversational session, Donnie digs into ontological concepts and how they might relate to categorizing design resources to evaluate usage in any particular experience. When we use the same units of measurement, how we communicate success across systems becomes consistent.

Joining Mike and Justin to break down what actually matters when you're building a product, not what looks impressive in a portfolio, but what builds trust with users and solves their actual problems. We get into keeping design systems coherent as products scale, the marketplace-of-components concept, and why functionality has to come before aesthetics.

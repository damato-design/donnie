A four-part conversation at Knapsack's Patterns event in NYC:

- **Meaning vs. momentum** — what meaningful progress with AI actually looks like, and where the untapped, unsexy, or misunderstood opportunities are.
- **Integration, readiness, and the work ahead** — the data, governance, and cultural foundations that make AI adoption sustainable rather than experimental, and how it becomes part of the operating model.
- **Design systems in an AI-driven world** — how systems built for consistency evolve when interfaces become generative, what it takes to make "brand" computable, and whether a design system can teach an AI to design responsibly.
- **Authenticity and the human role** — if AI can generate anything, what makes something worth making, how to preserve authorship and brand voice, and which instincts keep people essential to the creative process.

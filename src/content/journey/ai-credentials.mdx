To ground my work on AI in design systems, I completed several certifications in April 2026:

- **LLM Fine-Tuning and Customization Training**, Simplilearn
- **AI Ethics**, Alex Genadinik
- **Avoiding AI Harm**, Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center
- **Crafting AI Identities: Strategies & Ethical Considerations**, Coursera

The throughline is responsible, intentional AI: not just what these systems can generate, but what they should, and how that maps onto the governance and brand questions design systems already grapple with. This study runs alongside building the [D'Amato Design MCP server](/projects/mcp).

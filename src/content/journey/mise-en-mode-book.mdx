After years of research and a technique first shared at Clarity 2023, Mise en Mode became a book. Structured in three parts, Concept, Develop, and Future, it presents an intent-based approach that replaces token bloat with modes that express context, and works through practical implementation with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript across multi-brand and inclusive design challenges.

Find it at [mode.place](https://mode.place).

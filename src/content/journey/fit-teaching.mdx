At FIT, I work with students who often arrive from adjacent creative disciplines and are applying that sensibility to digital products for the first time. The courses move from framing a research question and recruiting participants, through synthesizing findings into wireframes, and on to clickable prototypes ready for evaluation, with a focus on building the vocabulary and confidence to defend design decisions with evidence.

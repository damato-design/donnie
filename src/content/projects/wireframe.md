Most design conversations happen behind closed doors; in critiques, Slack threads, and meetings that never get shared with the wider community. Wireframe is an attempt to open some of those conversations up. It's a weekday live show where I sit down to talk through best practices in our industry, explore new ways of approaching experiences, and occasionally land on a spicier take than I planned to.

The show is built around guests with a provocative point of view in design or development, and the format intentionally keeps things loose. Episodes stream live on YouTube and are repackaged into short-form clips and an audio podcast on your regular platforms, so folks who can't tune in at 5pm ET can still follow along. The goal is less to publish polished thought leadership and more to make the unscripted, in-progress version of design talk easier to find.
